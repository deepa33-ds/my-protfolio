Blog

Treking

24 Feb, 2017

Langtang and Mustang were among the most unforgettable trips I’ve ever taken. From the remote, mystical landscapes of Mustang to the lush forests and peaceful villages of Langtang, every moment felt like stepping into a living postcard.

Mustang – The Hidden Kingdom

Mustang was my first stop. The journey through Kali Gandaki Valley, the stone-walled village of Marpha, and the barren beauty of Lo Manthang were awe-inspiring. The windswept desert-like terrain, ancient caves, and Tibetan culture made me feel like I had traveled back in time.

Langtang – Nature’s Sanctuary

Langtang offered a peaceful contrast. I trekked through dense rhododendron forests, crossed hanging bridges over roaring rivers, and stayed in welcoming Tamang villages. Kyanjin Gompa, surrounded by snow-capped peaks, was a highlight – a place so tranquil and spiritual, it felt magical.

The Journey – A Blend of Adventure and Serenity

Whether it was sipping apple cider in Marpha or watching yaks graze in Langtang Valley, every day held something special. The breathtaking mountain views, warm-hearted locals, and cultural richness of both regions made this trip a soul-refreshing adventure.

I’d go back in a heartbeat to relive the silence of the Himalayas and the charm of Nepal’s untouched beauty.

AttendEase – Facial Recognition Attendance System

09 July, 2025

🔧 Project Overview

This video showcases a Facial Recognition Attendance System developed as part of my BSc CSIT 7th Semester project. The system automates student attendance using real-time face detection and recognition through a GUI built with Tkinter, backed by a MySQL database.

✅ Key Features

  • Student registration with photo samples
  • Real-time face detection and recognition
  • Automatic attendance marking
  • MySQL database integration for record storage
  • Admin panel for managing students and attendance reports

🔧 Tools & Technologies

  • Language: Python
  • Libraries: OpenCV (Face Detection & Recognition), Tkinter (GUI)
  • Database: MySQL
  • Algorithm: Haar Cascade Classifier

📁 Modules Covered

  • Student Registration
  • Dataset Collection
  • Model Training
  • Face Recognition
  • Attendance Logging
  • Admin Panel
